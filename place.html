Geographical Location

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is located at Koovappally, Kanjirappally in Kottayam district, Kerala. The campus is situated in a calm and green environment, away from city congestion, which provides an ideal atmosphere for academic learning and personal development.

Accessibility & Connectivity

The college is well connected by road transport and is easily accessible from nearby towns such as Kanjirappally, Ponkunnam, and Erattupetta. Regular bus services operate from Kottayam, Changanassery, and nearby transport hubs, making daily commuting convenient for students and staff.

Campus Infrastructure

AJCE has a spacious and well-planned campus with modern academic blocks, laboratories, libraries, hostels, sports facilities, and dining areas. The student-friendly campus layout ensures smooth service delivery and efficient movement between departments.

Mode of Service Delivery

  • Traditional offline classroom teaching
  • Blended learning with online platforms
  • Learning Management System (LMS) for notes, assignments, and exams
  • Virtual labs, webinars, and online workshops
  • Flexible academic schedules and mentoring support

Residential & Support Facilities

The college provides separate hostels for boys and girls with dining, internet access, and security. Additional facilities such as counseling, medical care, transportation services, and placement support enhance the overall student experience.
